Bookish Highlights

Here are some of my personal favourite Bookish Highlights!

  • The Hay Festival
    The town Hay-on-Wye in Wales is the first official 'Book Town' and is filled to the brim with bookshops. During the Hay Festival, authors and speakers from all over the world come to this town to give talks, sign books and celebrate reading. I've been twice now, and think it's absolutely magical!
  • The National Library of Wales
    I visited the National Library of Wales in Aberystwyth two years ago, and was able to see special exhibitions on the development of the dictionary, books with beautiful covers, and surprising art collections as well.
  • Athenaeumbibliotheek
    This library in Deventer is the oldest city library of the Netherlands, founded in 1560. During the Open Monument Weekend, I could view their special collections.
  • Librije
    This small library sits in the corner of the Walburgis church in Zutphen, and is one of the only chained libraries in the world that is still in its original state since 1562. It contains early printed books, such as the one on display from 1477.
