Get to know me:

My name is Nina de Rijk, I am 23 years old and I was born and raised on a little island at the bottom of South Holland, called Goeree Overflakkee. After living here for most of my life, I moved to Tilburg for a little bit, and am currently residing in Rotterdam. This is also where I plan to stay for the foreseeable future.

I did not enjoy this at all, so after receiving my Propedeuse, I quit the program and moved back home.

During this bachelor program, I did a semester abroad at the University of Leeds. During this semester, I followed three courses at the School of English I enjoyed this immensely, and loved living abroad.
